ABOUT US

Broadway Roastery offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other in Saskatoon. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that) , we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you.

If you needs to rest, we have a soft-seating area that is perfect for your weary mind and body. We offer a delicious variety of coffee made our trained baristas. We haave everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverage. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at the Broadway Roastery!
