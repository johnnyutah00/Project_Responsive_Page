Iced Coffee Summer Sale

By: Broadway Roastery Owner On May 25, 2018
Are you ready for summer? So are we!

The Broadway Roastery will be having an iced coffee summer sale at both of our locations in Saskatoon from June 1 until August 31. During this time, any order for a Vietnamese, Cappuccino, or Frappe coffee drink will only be $3.60 instead of the usual $4.60.

We would like to thank all of our loyal customers for making this sale possible. Have a nice summer everyone!
